# Product Vision

## Problem
People who run personal advisory calls often lose demand because inbound requests arrive through scattered DMs, email, and forms with inconsistent context.

## Target User
- Primary user: independent advisor / consultant / coach with recurring inbound session requests
- Secondary user: visitors who want a low-friction way to request time or guidance

## Why Now
Lightweight async intake plus AI-assisted triage can reduce admin overhead before a full scheduling product exists.

## Product Promise
Turn messy inbound interest into a structured, triageable request pipeline in minutes.

## Non-goals
- full CRM
- payments in v1
- multi-admin organization support
